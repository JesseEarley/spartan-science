What is Spartan Science?

Spartan Science is the official magazine of MSU’s College of Natural Science, dedicated to shining a spotlight on trail‑blazing research, compelling student and faculty stories, and the transformative impact of science on society. Each issue offers in-depth features on discoveries in fields such as biotechnology, neuroscience, ecology, and computational science highlighted by interviews with Spartan researchers, hands‑on lab updates, and opportunities for undergraduate involvement.

Readers enjoy:

  • Cutting‑edge research spotlights — from groundbreaking climate models to advancements in gene editing and materials science.
  • Student perspectives — stories from undergraduates and graduate students, sharing their academic journeys, internships, competitions, and lab life.
  • Community & outreach highlights — initiatives like STEM education programs, local environmental partnerships, and demonstration events showcasing how science empowers communities.
  • Science news and innovation — summaries of recent findings, patents, and university‑industry collaborations that demonstrate how Spartan discoveries reach the classroom and the marketplace.

Whether you're a prospective student curious about the sciences, a current Spartan looking for inspiration, or an alum staying connected, Spartan Science delivers insights into how Michigan State is fueling curiosity, cultivating innovation, and shaping the future.
